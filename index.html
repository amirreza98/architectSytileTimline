Periods

Eight eras, one thread: structure, space, and society.

  1. Megalithic

    c. 3000–2000 BCE

    Stone circles, trilithons, post-and-lintel structures.

  2. Ancient Egypt

    c. 2700–1070 BCE

    Pyramids, hypostyle halls, axial composition.

  3. Classical Greece

    c. 600–323 BCE

    Orders, proportion systems, entasis.

  4. Roman

    27 BCE–476 CE

    Arch, vault, dome—concrete revolution.

  5. Gothic

    12th–16th c.

    Ribbed vaults, pointed arches, flying buttresses.

  6. Renaissance

    15th–16th c.

    Humanist geometry, central plans, domes.

  7. Modern

    20th c.

    Frame + curtain wall, minimalism, new materials.

  8. Contemporary

    Late 20th–21st c.

    Parametric forms, sustainability, composites.
